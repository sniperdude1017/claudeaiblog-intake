What We Collect

Information submitted through the site

If you use the update form, the site may collect the information you type into that form, including your name, email address, and any optional phone number you choose to share.

If you contact the site by email, the information in that message may also be received and used to respond to your request.

Visit Information

The site may also receive standard visit information when you use it, such as the page you visited, the site or link that referred you, and campaign information present in the URL.

Cookies and Storage

Browser storage and measurement tools

The site may use cookies or similar browser storage to remember visit context, understand whether a form was completed, and help operate the signup flow.

Analytics or advertising measurement tools may also be used from time to time to understand site performance and traffic sources.

How We Use It

How information may be used

Sharing

When information may be shared

Information may be shared with service providers that help host, operate, analyze, or communicate from the site when that is needed to run the service.

Information may also be disclosed when reasonably necessary to comply with law, enforce terms, or protect the site and its operator.
